Tema 1: The stranger

Hi friends, please help me to have some example data for my storytelling database. Briefly, I'm trying to make a website to support and inspire writers (writing teams) in their storytelling projects. User contributions (writers') will randomly mixed together to create unpredictable stories. Each generated story can provide initial unexpected insights, ideas, and inspirations that the writing team and project managers can develop further. It is not going to be a traditional work environment, but a space to experiment and discover new narrative directions.

Please answer the following questions based on the images below. You can write as much as you want. Start the sentence with capital letter and finish with full stop. Try to connect your answers/sentences connect with each other as fluidly as possible as if you're writing a one story out of these questions. You don't have to spend much time and try hard to make it perfect. This is only example data that I could use to build my website and experiment with the random mixing logic. Your answers will be sent to my email. Thanks a lot for your help!

PS: I wrote the explanation in English because it was faster for me to write, but if you speak Italian, please answer in Italian.

Domanda 1

Cosa spinge il soggetto a camminare continuamente in questo ambiente?

What drives the subject to walk continously in this place?

Domanda 2

Cosa vede il soggetto quando alza lo sguardo? Chi o cosa sono le figure indistinte? Come si sente il soggetto nel vederle?

What does the subject see when he/she looks up? Who or what are those vague figures? How does he/she feels seeing them?

Domanda 3

Il punto di vista cambia. Cosa suggerisce l'inquadratura?

The point of view changes. What does this shot could mean?

Domanda 4

Perché il soggetto accelera il passo? Cosa lo sta spingendo ad andare più veloce?

Why does the subject start going faster?

Domanda 5

Dove sta andando il soggetto mentre sale le scale?

Where is the subject going as he/she climbs up the stairs?

Domanda 6

Cosa pensa o spera il soggetto mentre bussa freneticamente alla porta? Chi o cosa potrebbe essere dietro quella porta?

What does the subject think or hope while knocking hurriedly on the door? Who or what could be behind that door?

Domanda 7

Cosa potrebbe succedere una volta che la porta si apre? Quali emozioni o eventi potrebbe rivelare l'apertura?

What could happen once the door opens? What emotions or events could be waiting for him/her?
