About me

Hello, I'm Zaya!

When I was a little girl in kindergarten, I discovered that i enjoy drawing. The hobby quickly grew into a passion for anything that is creative. It lead me to explore more and self-teach from all the sources available out there. I'm eager to challenge myself by experimenting with as many art forms as possible: mixing different techniques and mediums, paper crafts, clay creations, embroidery, crochet and painting... In my opinion, creative process of art not only helps with one's artistic skills, but with the capacity for concentration and reduces stress and anxiety.

At the age of 14 I started my online mini-business of hand-crafted goods in my home country Mongolia. Today, since I'm currently living and studying in Italy, I thought that it was a good idea to reopen my shop available in several European countries and Mongolia, after receiving some requests from people who wanted to buy my designs here too. You can find loads of options in my online shop such as:

  • Traditional art prints
  • Digital art prints
  • Photography prints
  • Handmade clay earrings

Don't hesitate to leave me a message if you have any questions or would like to work together!
